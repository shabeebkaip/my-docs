Capabilities

How the Agent Actually Works

Three intelligent modules — working in concert to eliminate manual procurement overhead entirely.

📊
01
BOQ Intelligence
Upload any Excel or PDF BOQ. The AI parses divisions, line items, quantities and interprets what materials need to be procured — exactly as a senior procurement engineer would.
  • Handles merged cells and multi-sheet workbooks
  • Understands non-standard and legacy formats
  • Division-level breakdown and grouping
  • Automatic quantity and unit validation
🔌
02
Odoo 19 Integration
Every procurement list the agent generates is pushed into Odoo 19 as live Purchase Requisition records. Products verified, vendors matched, records created — zero manual entry.
  • Live bidirectional Odoo 19 sync
  • Products cross-referenced against your catalogue
  • Vendor matching from your approved list
  • Full audit trail within Odoo
📡
03
Procurement Tracker
The agent doesn't stop at creation. It monitors each requisition in Odoo — flagging what's ordered, pending, and overdue. Automated alerts surface delays before they become site problems.
  • Per-item real-time status visibility
  • Automated delay alerts and escalation
  • Dashboard overview across all projects
  • Historical reporting and analytics

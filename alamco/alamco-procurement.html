Meet Ahmed, a procurement engineer at a Riyadh construction firm. He just received a Bill of Quantities for a new office building project—120 items ranging from furniture to lighting. Traditionally, this would take him 2-3 days of manual work. Watch what happens when he uses the AI Procurement System—complete procurement in just 2-4 hours...

1
9:00 AM · Monday
Ahmed uploads the BOQ file
He drags and drops the Excel file containing 120 line items. Within seconds, the AI BOQ Parser springs into action—reading every row, extracting descriptions, quantities, specifications.
What happened
120 items parsed • Categorized into Furniture, Lighting, HVAC • Specifications standardized • Ready for matching
2
9:01 AM · 60 seconds later
AI discovers suppliers across Saudi Arabia
While Ahmed grabs his coffee, the Supplier Discovery Engine is already working—scanning the database, scraping the web, finding distributors, wholesalers, and manufacturers who carry these exact products.
Suppliers found
47 qualified vendors • 18 in Riyadh • 12 in Jeddah • 17 in Dammam • All verified and rated
3
9:02-9:30 AM · The outreach begins
AI agents start contacting suppliers
This is where the magic happens. AIKA (AI voice agent) starts calling suppliers. AIWA (WhatsApp agent) sends messages. Emails go out automatically. All personalized, all simultaneous, all professional. The system handles 32 outreach attempts in just 30 minutes.
Outreach complete
32 suppliers contacted • 18 via phone calls • 10 via WhatsApp • 4 via email • Now waiting for responses
4
11:30 AM · Quotes are pouring in
AI collects and compares all quotes
Over the next 2 hours, suppliers respond via calls, WhatsApp, and emails. The Quote Comparison Engine automatically extracts prices, delivery times, and terms as they arrive. Everything is standardized and compared in real-time on Ahmed's dashboard.
Analysis complete
25 quotes received • Best prices identified • 22% below budget • 3-day delivery available • Savings: 47,800 SAR
✓
1:00 PM · Mission accomplished
Ahmed approves. Purchase orders generated.
After lunch, Ahmed reviews the comparison dashboard with all 25 quotes organized and analyzed. He clicks "Approve Best Quotes," and the system automatically generates purchase orders for each supplier. POs are sent via email and WhatsApp. Job done—all before end of business.
Final result
Total time: 4 hours • 120 items sourced • 25 suppliers compared • 47,800 SAR saved • What used to take 2-3 days done in one morning

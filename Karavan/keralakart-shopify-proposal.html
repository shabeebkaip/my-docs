Project Understanding
3.1 Current Situation
KeralaKart currently receives orders via WhatsApp, collects payments via Interac e-Transfer, and handles dispatch and follow-up manually. This process has validated customer demand, but it cannot scale and creates unnecessary operational load on the team.
3.2 What This Project Delivers vs. What's Out of Scope
✓ Phase 1 — Included
✅Professional online store at keralakart.ca — browse, cart, checkout independently
✅Shopify Payments — Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay, credit/debit
✅Up to 60 Karavan SKUs — organised, searchable, with full product data
✅Automated order confirmation & shipping notifications
✅GTA shipping zones + Ontario HST correctly configured
✅Clean admin panel for orders, stock, and product management
✅Admin training + fulfilment SOP document
✕ Out of Scope — Phase 1
❌Mobile app (iOS or Android) — available in Phase 2
❌Klaviyo advanced email/SMS automation flows
❌WhatsApp Business API integration (WATI / Interakt)
❌QuickBooks Online / accounting integration (Synder)
❌Custom theme development — premium theme branded instead
❌Ongoing maintenance retainer — available separately
Future Growth Path

This Phase 1 store is intentionally designed as a launchpad. When KeralaKart is ready to scale — with a mobile app, marketing automation, or a full Odoo ERP backend — Code-Ox will be ready to take the next step. No rebuild required.

5
Unanswered Questions — Confirm Before Demo

Both respondents left the following questions blank. The demo can proceed with the suggested defaults, but these should be confirmed with the Vitality team to avoid presenting the wrong behaviour.

Q4 — How do patients currently request a home visit?
Not answered by either respondent. Demo will show WhatsApp as the primary intake channel. Ask: Do patients call the clinic directly, or use an app/website?
Q7 — How should therapists be assigned?
Demo default: Assign based on (1) proximity to patient location, (2) specialization match for the requested service, (3) current availability. Confirm if any other criteria apply (e.g. preferred therapist, gender preference).
Q8 — Wait time before notifying next therapist?
Demo default: 10 minutes. If therapist does not accept within 10 minutes, AI notifies the next available therapist. Confirm if a different window is preferred.
Q9 — What types of files might patients send?
Demo default: PDF (prescriptions, medical reports, discharge summaries), JPEG/PNG (wound photos, X-ray images, referral letters). Confirm if other formats are needed.
Q10 — What should the AI do with patient-sent files?
Demo default: (1) Acknowledge receipt to patient via WhatsApp. (2) Attach file to the patient's booking record. (3) Notify coordinator that a document has been received and is awaiting review.
Q11 — How far in advance should reminders be sent?
Demo default: Two reminders — 24 hours before the appointment and 2 hours before. Confirm preferred timing windows.
Q12 — How should reminders be sent?
Demo default: WhatsApp message (primary). Optionally, voice call as secondary (Respondent 1 uses phone calls currently). Confirm if SMS should also be included.
Q14 — Existing HIS / scheduling system?
Not answered. Demo will use dummy data only (confirmed by both). Ask: Is there an existing system the live version would need to integrate with? Not needed for the demo itself.

Suggested Demo Script (Walkthrough Order)

Recommended order for presenting the demo to the Vitality team. Each scene maps to a confirmed use case.

Scene What to Show Maps To
Scene 1 Patient messages in English: "I need a physiotherapist at home tomorrow morning." AI collects location, date/time, confirms service. Assigns nearest therapist. Therapist accepts. Booking confirmed. UC-1, UC-2
Scene 2 Same flow in Arabic: "أريد ممرضة في المنزل." AI switches to Arabic automatically and completes the booking. UC-1, UC-2 (AR)
Scene 3 Patient sends a prescription PDF and a wound photo via WhatsApp. AI acknowledges receipt and flags coordinator. UC-4
Scene 4 Show the automated reminder being sent to patient 24 hours before the appointment. UC-3
Scene 5 Therapist does not respond within 10 minutes. AI automatically notifies the next available therapist. After another failed attempt, AI escalates to staff. UC-5
Scene 6 Patient types "this is an emergency, I need someone now." AI detects urgency and immediately escalates to human coordinator. UC-6, UC-7

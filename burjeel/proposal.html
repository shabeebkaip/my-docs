09 — Risk & Mitigation

We've planned for it.

Four identified risks — each with a clear mitigation strategy built into our delivery process.

📄Delays in Content Delivery
Delays in receiving texts, staff photos, and brand assets can block design and development progress.
Mitigation
A content checklist is provided at kick-off. Placeholder content used during development to keep progress unblocked.
⚙️Technical Downtime or Booking Errors
Post-launch system downtime or booking errors could damage patient experience and brand trust.
Mitigation
Reliable hosting recommended. Comprehensive pre-launch testing and UAT. 30-day monitoring and bug-fix support included.
📉Low Initial Booking Adoption
Patients may initially prefer phone or walk-in bookings, leading to underutilization post-launch.
Mitigation
In-branch promotion by front desk; website prompts and push notifications; digital marketing campaigns alongside launch.
👩‍💼Staff Training Requirements
Staff unfamiliar with the new system may create friction in handling online appointments.
Mitigation
Structured training sessions, user manual documentation, and dedicated support contact during the launch phase.

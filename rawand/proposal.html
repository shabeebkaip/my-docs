Where Rawand Stands Today
The Situation — Stronger Than It Looks

Live site inspection and Google search analysis reveals that Rawand has built a significantly stronger digital foundation than most Saudi B2B office equipment companies. Google Ads are running and placing Rawand as the #1 sponsored result for key queries. All major tracking pixels are installed. The Zid platform provides a professional, bilingual, mobile-optimized storefront. All five major social platforms are linked.

The opportunity is not about rebuilding — it is about activating what is already in place and filling the specific gaps that are costing Rawand revenue every single day.

What Is Working ✓
Google Search Ads: #1 sponsored result for "hp printer laserjet" — ahead of Jarir and Azra IT Systems.

Tracking infrastructure: Facebook, TikTok, Snapchat pixels + GTM all active.

Payment stack: STCPay, Mada, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, Tamara BNPL, Tabby BNPL.

Product depth: 10 ink brands, 350+ products, Ricoh + Konica Minolta + Canon imagePROGRAF.

Category pages: Each has a banner image, description, and brand sub-navigation.

Social platforms: Instagram, X, Snapchat, TikTok, Facebook all in footer.

Unique differentiator: Maintenance & repair services — no major competitor offers this.

SEO — Fix the Foundation, Build the Authority
Technical fixes + bilingual content hub + local SEO domination
+50% Organic Traffic in 90 days
Rawand's SEO score is 4.8/10. The critical failures — missing hreflang tags, no canonical tags, no meta descriptions — are preventing Google from correctly indexing either the EN or AR version. These are technically simple fixes with an outsized ranking impact. The category pages already have descriptions and breadcrumbs; the technical SEO just needs to connect the dots.
Add hreflang tags to all pages (EN-SA + AR-SA + x-default)
Add canonical tags site-wide — stops duplicate content
Write 350 meta descriptions (all product + category pages)
Fix H1 on EN homepage; fix multiple H1s on AR homepage
Add Open Graph tags — fix broken social sharing
Allow AI crawler bots (GPTBot, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot)
Fix robots.txt — unblock /js/ directory
Add "Riyadh" + "Saudi Arabia" to all titles and H1s
Google Business Profile — photos, hours, 50+ reviews, products
Promote offline store (Al-Hawari, Riyadh) on Maps + Meta + Snap
Standardize NAP: Al-Hawari, Riyadh across all listings
Meta + TikTok + Snapchat "Visit Our Store" local ads targeting Riyadh radius
Add Product schema + FAQPage schema + BlogPosting schema

2
GEO — Own AI Search Before Competitors Discover It
ChatGPT · Perplexity · Google AI Overviews — all citing Rawand
Blue Ocean — 0 competitors
GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the practice of making your brand appear when someone asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI "who sells HP printers in Riyadh?" Currently, no Saudi office equipment supplier is optimized for this. AI traffic grew 1,300% in the 2024 holiday season and converts 31% higher than organic. This is a 2–3 year window before it becomes competitive.
Create Wikidata entity for Rawand Trading Company
Add FAQPage schema to all category and product pages
Create /llms.txt at site root with plain-text brand description
Get listed on HP.com, Canon.com, Ricoh Saudi "Where to Buy"
Press mention in Arab News, Argaam.com, Zawya
Answer questions on Reddit / Muqawil about printer suppliers
Launch YouTube channel — cited by ChatGPT + Perplexity
Structure blog with direct answers in first 40–60 words
Target: Appear in 5 AI queries (Month 3)
20+ AI search queries (Month 6)

Maintenance Services — Build Rawand's Recurring Revenue Moat
The only B2B differentiator no marketplace can copy — dramatically undersold today

Create tiered service packages: Per-visit, Monthly, Annual

Cross-Sell, Reviews & Retention — Maximize Every Customer
Average order value up · Repeat purchase rate up · Google Shopping rank up
+20–35% average order value
Three high-impact, low-cost improvements that compound over time: (1) Cross-sell logic ensures every printer buyer sees compatible toner, and every ink buyer sees a maintenance service offer. (2) A review collection campaign targets past customers via WhatsApp — 100 product reviews in 30 days dramatically improves Google Shopping ranking and conversion rate. (3) A reorder automation cycle ensures ink and toner buyers are reminded to repurchase at exactly the right time.
Configure "related products" in Zid for top 50 SKUs
WhatsApp review request to all past customers (offer 5% off next order)
Set up toner reorder reminder (Day 45, 60, 90 post-purchase)
Add maintenance service cross-sell to all copier product pages
Add OG tags — fix broken social sharing for all products
Respond to all reviews in Arabic and English within 24 hours
Target: 100 product reviews (30 days)
300+ reviews (6 months)
90-day repurchase rate: 35%+

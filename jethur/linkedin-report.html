Product — RootX Platform

RootX is Jethur's unified GRC backbone — a single data model where every organizational element connects: controls, risks, policies, assets, third parties, incidents, BIAs, KRIs, and evidence. Built on an API-first, microservice architecture with cloud and on-premises deployment options.

High Priority 📝 1 post/week · High leverage

Medium Priority 📰 Bi-weekly · Medium effort

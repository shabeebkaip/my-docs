Outreach Templates
The 3-Message Sequence

Each message is personalised to the prospect's role and context. These templates show the strategic arc — from building rapport to opening a conversation.

1
Day 0 — Connection Request Note
Personalised Connection
Hi [Name], I noticed your work at [Company] — especially around [compliance/risk area]. We're building Jethur, an AI-powered GRC platform built for regulated industries across MENA and South Asia, covering frameworks like SAMA, NCA, RBI, and DPDP. Would love to connect and share what we're seeing in the regional compliance space.
2
Day 3–5 — Value Message (after acceptance)
Insight Share
Thanks for connecting, [Name]. We recently published a breakdown of the [PDPL/DPDP/NCA/SAMA/RBI] changes affecting [their industry] across [their region] — happy to share it directly if useful. We've also put together a short overview of how teams like yours are using RootX to cut audit prep time significantly. Worth a look?
3
Day 10–12 — Soft Ask
Meeting Request
Hey [Name], last message — I know you're busy. If [compliance challenge] is something on your radar for 2026, I'd genuinely enjoy a 20-minute call to show you what RootX does and get your take on it. No sales pressure — just a conversation. Would any time next week work?

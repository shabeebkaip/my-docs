Overview
Executive Summary
Code-ox Technologies is pleased to submit this commercial quotation for the design and development of the Vitality Home Healthcare corporate website. Based on the Business Requirements Document (BRD) presented by Albandry Alotaibi, this proposal covers the full scope of UI/UX design, frontend and backend development, third-party integrations, bilingual implementation, and go-live support.

Our solution will deliver a patient-focused, high-performance digital platform that converts visitors into confirmed bookings through an intuitive, physician-led presentation and a streamlined, minimal-step online booking journey.

Project Timeline
Delivery Plan — 8 Weeks
WEBSITE & BOOKING SYSTEM
1
Week 1
Kickoff & Discovery
  • Project kickoff meeting with Vitality Home Healthcare stakeholders — goals, scope, and expectations aligned
  • Target audience, patient personas, and key user journeys defined across the website
  • Content brief issued to client — required assets, service descriptions, medical team bios, and imagery
  • Technical architecture confirmed — hosting environment, CMS platform, domain, and third-party tools
  • Project workspace set up — task tracker, shared drive, and weekly check-in schedule established
2
Week 2
UX Wireframing & Design System
  • UX wireframes produced for all key pages — homepage, service pages, programs & packages, promotions, medical team, about, news, and booking flow
  • Mobile-first layout planning with responsive breakpoints defined for all device sizes
  • Design system created — typography, colour palette, button styles, card components, and iconography
  • Bilingual (AR/EN) layout structure defined — RTL and LTR switching verified at wireframe stage
  • Design prototype presented for client approval; sign-off obtained before development begins
3
Weeks 3–4
Core Website Development
  • Development environment configured — staging server, version control (Git), and deployment pipeline
  • All pages built to approved designs: homepage, about, services & detail pages, programs, promotions, medical team, testimonials, news & media, and footer
  • CMS integrated — admin panel configured, content types structured, editor roles assigned
  • Cross-browser testing (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) and multi-device testing completed
  • Image optimisation, lazy loading, and Core Web Vitals baseline verified
4
Week 5
Bilingual Implementation, Content & SEO
  • Full RTL/LTR switching implemented — Arabic and English language toggle live across all pages
  • All client-provided content migrated and formatted within the CMS across all pages
  • On-page SEO applied — page titles, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, and image alt text
  • Healthcare schema markup added (MedicalBusiness, Physician, Service) for rich search results
  • XML sitemap generated and submitted; robots.txt configured for search engine crawling
  • Google Analytics 4 and Search Console integrated and ownership verified

Online Booking Flow & Payment Gateway Integration

Operations Modules

🍽️ Menu Management
→ Plans & Plan Categories
Create/edit plans, set meal count, day choices, plan choices, and categorize for filtering
→ Meals & Meal Categories
Set price, rating, status, kitchen type, plans, tags, macros, ingredients, recipe, available days
→ Ingredients
Create/edit ingredients, link to meals, manage stock and nutrition info
→ Ingredient Categories
Group ingredients into categories for organisation
🚚 Delivery & Logistics
→ Drivers
Add/edit driver info: name, phone, shifts, gender, service zones
→ Driver Orders
Assign orders to drivers, view item lists, print delivery sheets, filter by date/driver, track status
→ Zones, Areas & Blocks
Create delivery zones → areas → blocks with drivers, shifts, and delivery times
→ Shifts
Manage delivery shifts, set timings, assign to drivers and areas
👥 Customers & Dietitians
→ Customer Tags
Create and manage tags for customer segmentation and filtering
→ Dietitians
Create/edit profiles, contact info, specialization, schedule appointments, assign to customers, view consultation history

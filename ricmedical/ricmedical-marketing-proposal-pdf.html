01  ·  Executive Summary
Setting the Strategic Direction

RIC Medical operates at the intersection of global medical innovation and Saudi Arabia's rapidly expanding healthcare sector. This document presents a structured digital marketing strategy designed to elevate RIC's market visibility, engage clinical decision-makers, and generate qualified leads for the sales team.

The strategy is anchored in two featured technologies — the DRX9000 Spinal Decompression System by Excite Medical and Redpine Single-Use Endoscopy Solutions — while positioning RIC Medical as a broad-portfolio technology partner serving hospitals, clinics, and healthcare institutions across the Kingdom.

Rather than a fragmented series of campaigns, this blueprint delivers a unified, multi-platform digital presence that builds trust at every touchpoint in the B2B healthcare buyer journey — from initial discovery through to a sales conversation.

🎯

Primary Objective

Position RIC Medical as a trusted healthcare technology partner and generate qualified leads across key clinical segments in Saudi Arabia.

📡

Channel Approach

LinkedIn for B2B lead generation, Instagram for brand credibility, TikTok for educational discovery and awareness across clinical audiences.

🏥

Market Focus

Orthopedics, spine care, pain management, gastroenterology, hospital procurement, and surgical centers across the Kingdom.

02  ·  Healthcare Market Context
How Hospitals Discover Medical Technology Today

The way hospitals and clinicians evaluate and procure medical technology has fundamentally shifted. Decision-making is no longer confined to trade shows and in-person sales calls — it begins online, often before a supplier is ever contacted.

The Digital Research Reality

Orthopedic surgeons research device efficacy on PubMed and LinkedIn. Gastroenterologists watch procedural technique videos on social platforms. Procurement managers cross-reference suppliers on Google before initiating contact. Hospital administrators evaluate technology partners based on their digital presence and perceived credibility.

In Saudi Arabia, Vision 2030's healthcare transformation agenda is accelerating the adoption of advanced medical technologies. New hospitals, clinic expansions, and private-sector healthcare growth are creating significant demand for trusted medical technology partners.

Key Market Insight

A medical technology supplier without a coherent digital presence is effectively invisible to a growing segment of clinical decision-makers who begin their vendor evaluation online. Digital presence is no longer optional — it is a prerequisite for commercial credibility.

03  ·  Featured Technologies
Two Pillars of Modern Healthcare Technology

While RIC Medical distributes a broad portfolio of advanced medical solutions, two technologies are designated as the initial marketing focus — representing the most strategically positioned products to open doors and drive early commercial momentum.

Featured Technology 01

DRX9000

Specialty Treatment Technology

The DRX9000 by Excite Medical is a clinically validated, FDA-cleared non-surgical spinal decompression system. It delivers targeted therapeutic distraction to the lumbar spine, reducing intradiscal pressure and promoting healing in patients with chronic spinal conditions — without surgery or prolonged medication.

Indicated for:
Chronic Low Back Pain Herniated Discs Degenerative Disc Disease Sciatica Failed Back Surgery Syndrome
Primary Customers:
Orthopedic Surgeons Spine Specialists Physiotherapy Clinics Pain Management Centers
Featured Technology 02

Redpine Endoscopy

Diagnostic & Procedural Technology

Redpine offers a comprehensive range of single-use endoscopy systems that eliminate the risk of cross-contamination while reducing reprocessing costs. Their portfolio spans upper and lower GI diagnostics through to urological interventions — delivering hospital-grade performance in a disposable format.

Products Include:
Single-Use Gastroscopes Single-Use Colonoscopes Cystoscopes Ureteroscopes
Primary Customers:
Gastroenterologists Hospital Endoscopy Depts. Surgical Centers Urology Departments

11  ·  LinkedIn Lead Generation Strategy
Where Commercial Relationships Begin

LinkedIn is the primary commercial engine of this strategy — the only major social platform where healthcare professionals, hospital decision-makers, and procurement teams are directly accessible for professional engagement.

🏢

RIC Medical Company Page

Fully optimised with brand messaging and consistent content publishing. Acts as the institutional face of RIC and provides credibility validation when prospects research the company.

👤

BDM Personal Profile

The Business Development Manager's profile is the primary outreach vehicle. All commercial connections, messages, and conversations are conducted through the BDM — not the company page.

Why People Over Brands

Decision-makers respond significantly better to messages from a real person than from a corporate account. The company page builds institutional credibility; the BDM's profile generates the actual leads.

15  ·  90-Day Marketing Roadmap
A Phased Approach to Launch and Growth

The roadmap builds the marketing foundation correctly before accelerating into lead generation. Skipping the setup phase in favour of immediate outreach is a common error — a weak digital presence undermines the credibility of every sales conversation that follows.

1
Month 1 — Foundation & Setup
Platform Optimisation and Content Infrastructure
  • Optimise RIC Medical LinkedIn company page — branding, keywords, description, contact information
  • Optimise BDM LinkedIn personal profile — headline, about section, experience, featured content
  • Establish Instagram account with brand-consistent profile, bio, and initial content grid
  • Set up TikTok account and create initial 3–5 educational videos for publishing
  • Define brand colour palette, typography, and graphic template suite
  • Create content calendar for Months 2–3 and audit website SEO basics
  • Identify and build initial LinkedIn outreach target list of 100+ decision-makers
2
Month 2 — Content Growth & Audience Building
Consistent Publishing and Community Development
  • Publish LinkedIn posts at minimum 4x per week — technology insights, clinical education, company updates
  • Launch Instagram content schedule — 3–4 posts per week including Reels and carousels
  • Begin TikTok publishing — 3 educational videos per week targeting clinical topics
  • BDM begins LinkedIn outreach — 10–15 personalised connection requests per day
  • Begin engagement strategy — commenting on relevant industry posts to increase visibility
  • Monitor performance and identify top-performing post formats
3
Month 3 — Outreach Scaling & Lead Generation
Commercial Conversations and Pipeline Development
  • Scale LinkedIn outreach — BDM initiating 20+ conversations per week with qualified prospects
  • Convert LinkedIn conversations into product demonstration requests and introduction calls
  • Introduce LinkedIn paid promotion for selected high-performing content
  • Begin tracking leads: source of discovery, conversion stage, follow-up timeline
  • Review KPIs against targets and refine content strategy based on data
  • Present performance report to RIC Medical leadership with insights and recommendations

01 — Introduction

What this guide covers

This guide is written for the IT administrator responsible for managing the RIC Gateway website. It covers everything you need to keep the website's content up to date — without requiring any programming or technical development knowledge.

The RIC Gateway website is the official online presence of Riyadh International Corporation (RIC Medical), showcasing medical equipment from world-class manufacturers including Mindray, GE Healthcare, BASDA, Combat Medical, and others.

Your Role as Content Manager

As the website administrator, your day-to-day responsibilities include:

  • Adding, updating, and removing products from the website catalogue
  • Managing product categories (e.g., Imaging, Diagnostics, ICU Equipment)
  • Adding and updating manufacturer / partner profiles and logos
  • Editing page text and content on the Home, About, Services, and Contact pages
  • Reviewing customer enquiries and quotation requests from the website
💡
No coding needed
You do not need to write any code or contact a developer for these tasks. Everything is managed through the visual admin dashboard using simple forms and buttons.
🔒
Security reminder
Your admin login credentials are confidential. Do not share them with anyone outside the authorised team. Always log out when finished.

Logging In

  1. 1Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari).
  2. 2In the address bar, type www.ricmedical.com.sa/admin/login and press Enter.
  3. 3You will see the RIC Gateway login screen with two fields.
  4. 4Enter your Email Address in the first field.
  5. 5Enter your Password in the second field.
  6. 6Click the "Sign In" button.
  7. 7If your credentials are correct, you will be taken to the main dashboard automatically.
⚠️
Login Credentials
Your login details will be provided separately by the development team. Keep them private. The system will lock access for 15 minutes after 5 incorrect password attempts.

Logging Out

Always log out when finished, especially on a shared computer.

  1. 1Look at the bottom of the left sidebar navigation panel.
  2. 2Click the "Logout" button.
  3. 3You will be returned to the login screen. You are now safely logged out.

04 — Products

Managing Products

The Products section is where you manage all medical equipment listed on the RIC Gateway website. Each product has a name, description, images, specifications, and more.

Viewing All Products

  1. 1Click "Products" in the left sidebar.
  2. 2You will see a table of all products currently on the website.
  3. 3Search for a product by typing its name in the search bar at the top.
  4. 4Filter by category or partner using the dropdown menus.

Adding a New Product

  1. 1Click "Products" in the left sidebar.
  2. 2Click the "Add Product" button (top-right corner).
  3. 3Fill in the product details using the form. See the field reference table below.
  4. 4Upload product images by clicking the image upload area and selecting photos from your computer.
  5. 5Click "Save Product" or "Create" at the bottom when finished.

Product Fields Reference

Field NameWhat to EnterRequired?
Product TitleThe full name of the product (e.g., "Mindray DC-80 Ultrasound System")Required
DescriptionA clear explanation of what the product is and what it doesRequired
CategorySelect from the dropdown (e.g., Imaging, Diagnostics)Required
Sub-CategoryA more specific grouping under the main category, if applicableOptional
Partner / ManufacturerSelect the brand (e.g., Mindray, GE Healthcare)Required
Product TypeSelect whether it is a "Product" or "Solution"Required
Thumbnail ImageThe main image shown in the product list (upload from computer)Required
Product ImagesAdditional photos — you can upload multiple imagesOptional
FeaturesKey highlights or feature points (add as separate lines)Optional
SpecificationsTechnical specs (e.g., Weight: 12kg, Display: 15-inch LCD)Optional
Is Featured?Turn ON to show this product in featured/highlighted sectionsOptional
Is Active?ON = visible on website. OFF = hidden temporarily.Optional
SEO TitleTitle for search engines like Google (usually same as product title)Optional
SEO DescriptionBrief description for search results (1–2 sentences)Optional
🖼️
Image Upload Tips
Use JPG or PNG format. Minimum size: 800 × 600 pixels. Images are uploaded to the cloud automatically. You can upload multiple images per product to create a gallery.

Editing an Existing Product

  1. 1Go to Products in the sidebar.
  2. 2Find the product (use the search bar if needed).
  3. 3Click the Edit button (pencil icon) next to the product.
  4. 4Make your changes in the form.
  5. 5Click "Save" or "Update" to apply the changes.

Deleting a Product

  1. 1Go to Products in the sidebar.
  2. 2Find the product you want to remove.
  3. 3Click the Delete button (bin icon) next to the product.
  4. 4A confirmation message will appear — click "Confirm" or "Yes, Delete".
⚠️
Deletion is permanent
Once deleted, a product cannot be recovered. To temporarily hide a product, use the "Is Active" toggle instead of deleting it.
05 — Categories

Managing Categories

Categories help visitors find products by grouping similar items together — e.g., "Imaging", "Patient Monitoring", or "Critical Care".

Viewing Categories

  1. 1Click "Categories" in the left sidebar.
  2. 2You will see all existing categories listed.

Adding a New Category

  1. 1Click "Categories" in the left sidebar.
  2. 2Click the "Add Category" button.
  3. 3Fill in the category details (name, description, icon if required).
  4. 4Click "Save" to create the category.

Category Fields Reference

FieldWhat to EnterRequired?
Category NameName displayed on the website (e.g., "Patient Monitoring")Required
DescriptionBrief explanation of what products belong in this categoryOptional
IconA small symbol or image representing the categoryOptional
Display OrderA number controlling the order categories appear (1 = first)Optional
Is Active?Toggle ON to show this category on the websiteOptional

Editing or Deleting a Category

Click the Edit (pencil) icon to modify, or the Delete (bin) icon to remove a category.

⚠️
Important
Do not delete a category that still has products assigned to it. First move those products to another category, then delete the empty one.
06 — Partners

Managing Partners (Manufacturers)

Manage the manufacturers and suppliers whose products are listed on the website — Mindray, GE Healthcare, BASDA, Combat Medical, and others. Each partner has a profile page with logo, description, and related products.

Adding a New Partner

  1. 1Click "Partners" in the left sidebar.
  2. 2Click "Add Partner".
  3. 3Fill in the partner information using the form.
  4. 4Upload the partner's logo image.
  5. 5Click "Save" to create the partner profile.

Partner Fields Reference

FieldWhat to EnterRequired?
Partner NameThe manufacturer's full name (e.g., "Mindray Medical International")Required
LogoUpload the manufacturer's official logo image (PNG preferred)Required
DescriptionA short paragraph about the manufacturer — who they are, what they makeOptional
WebsiteThe manufacturer's official website (e.g., www.mindray.com)Optional
CountryCountry where the manufacturer is based (e.g., China, USA, Germany)Optional
CategoriesSelect which product categories this partner's products belong toOptional
Invert Logo ColorTurn ON if the logo appears invisible on white backgroundsOptional
🖼️
Logo Image Tip
Use PNG format with a transparent background for the cleanest look. Avoid logos with coloured backgrounds unless the brand requires it.

Editing or Deleting a Partner

Click the Edit (pencil) icon to update details, or Delete (bin) to remove a partner.

⚠️
Important
Deleting a partner does not delete their products. Products assigned to a deleted partner may display incorrectly. Reassign products to another partner before deleting.
07 — Page Content

Managing Page Content

Edit the text, headings, and information displayed on each website page — without touching any code. Changes appear live on the website immediately after saving.

💡
How content management works
The website is divided into "pages" and each page has multiple "sections". For example, the Home page has a Hero section (the big top banner), an About section, a Partners section, and so on. You select the page and section to edit, change the text, and save.

How to Edit Content

  1. 1Click "Content" in the left sidebar.
  2. 2You will see a list of all content sections grouped by page.
  3. 3Click the Edit (pencil) icon next to the section you want to change.
  4. 4Update the text fields in the form that appears.
  5. 5Click "Save" to publish the changes to the live website.
⚡
Changes go live immediately
When you save content changes, they appear on the public website right away. Double-check your text for spelling and accuracy before saving.
08 — Enquiries

Viewing Customer Enquiries

When visitors fill out a form on the website — general contact or product quotation request — their message is saved in the Enquiries section. An email notification is also sent automatically to the RIC Medical team.

Types of Enquiries

Enquiry TypeSourceWhat It Contains
Contact EnquiriesContact page formVisitor's name, email, phone, company, and message
Product Quotation Requests"Request a Quote" button on product pagesVisitor details + the specific product they're interested in

Viewing Enquiries

  1. 1Click "Enquiries" in the left sidebar.
  2. 2You will see two tabs — Contact Enquiries and Product Requests.
  3. 3Click on any enquiry row to view the full details of the message.

Enquiry Status

Each enquiry has a status to help you track what's been handled:

StatusMeaning
NewJust received — has not been viewed or responded to yet
ReadHas been opened and reviewed in the admin dashboard
RepliedThe customer has been responded to (you update this manually)
📧
Automatic Email Notifications
Every time a visitor submits a form, an automatic email is sent to:
ricmede@ricmedical.com.sa and chukri@ricmedical.com.sa

You can reply directly from your email client. The enquiry dashboard is for record-keeping and tracking.

10 — Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting & Support

Common Issues & Solutions

ProblemLikely CauseWhat to Do
Cannot log in — "Invalid credentials" error Wrong email or password Double-check your email and password. Ensure Caps Lock is off. If forgotten, contact the developer to reset.
Account locked — "Too many attempts" More than 5 failed login attempts Wait 15 minutes and try again. If urgent, contact technical support.
Logged out automatically Session expired (after 7 days of inactivity) This is normal. Simply log in again at the admin login page.
Image not uploading File too large or wrong format Ensure the image is JPG or PNG. Try compressing if over 5MB — use a free tool like TinyPNG.
Changes not appearing on the live website Browser cache is showing an old version Press Ctrl + Shift + R (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + R (Mac) to hard-refresh the page.
Product not showing after adding "Is Active" toggle is turned OFF Edit the product and make sure the "Is Active" toggle is ON, then save.
Enquiry emails not arriving Email may be in spam/junk folder Check your spam folder. The email comes from shabeebkaip@gmail.com — mark as "Not Spam". The enquiry is always visible in the admin dashboard.
Page shows error / blank screen Temporary server issue Refresh the page. If it persists after 5 minutes, contact technical support.

Before Contacting Support

To help the technical team resolve your issue faster, please have the following ready:

  • A description of what you were trying to do
  • What happened instead (the error message, if any)
  • A screenshot of the issue — press Win + Shift + S on Windows, or Cmd + Shift + 4 on Mac
  • The time and date when the problem occurred

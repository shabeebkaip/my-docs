COX-RIC-2026-001 Digital Marketing Services Agreement
01  ·  Preamble
Recitals & Definitions

This Digital Marketing Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of 5 May 2026 (the "Effective Date") by and between:

Code-OX Technologies LLP, a limited liability partnership duly registered and incorporated, having its registered office at UAQ Business Centre, Westhill, Kozhikode, Kerala — 673005, India, and a business address at Building No. 3239, Al Zubair Bin Al Awwam Street, Al Rawabi District, Riyadh – 14215, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider" or "Code-OX"); and

Riyadh International Corporation Equipment Service (hereinafter also referred to as "RIC Medical"), a company operating in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, engaged in the distribution of advanced medical technology solutions to hospitals and healthcare institutions across the Kingdom (hereinafter referred to as the "Client" or "RIC Medical").

Code-OX and RIC Medical are each referred to herein individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties".

02  ·  Definitions
Defined Terms
2.1   Services

"Services" means the digital marketing, social media management, content creation, search engine optimisation, business development support, and related professional services described in Schedule A of this Agreement.

2.2   Deliverables

"Deliverables" means all content, creative assets, reports, strategies, and other tangible work products produced by Code-OX in the performance of the Services.

2.3   Monthly Retainer

"Monthly Retainer" means the fixed monthly fee payable by RIC Medical to Code-OX in accordance with the payment schedule set out in Schedule B.

2.4   Advertising Budget

"Advertising Budget" means the separate funds allocated and paid directly by RIC Medical to third-party digital platforms (LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, Google) for paid promotion and sponsored content, distinct from the Monthly Retainer.

2.5   Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either Party to the other, including but not limited to business plans, client lists, pricing, trade secrets, marketing strategies, and technical information.

2.6   Intellectual Property

"Intellectual Property" or "IP" means all creative works, designs, content, code, strategies, reports, and any other original works of authorship produced under this Agreement.

Scope of Services

Code-OX shall provide the following Services to RIC Medical throughout the Term of this Agreement:

  • Development and execution of a comprehensive multi-platform social media strategy spanning LinkedIn, Instagram, and TikTok

  • LinkedIn Company Page optimisation and ongoing profile management
  • Monthly performance reports covering reach, engagement, follower growth, and platform analytics
3.2   Content Creation
  • Production of clinical education content for featured technologies: DRX9000 (Excite Medical) and Redpine Endoscopy Solutions
  • Design of branded graphic templates for posts, carousels, product highlights, and event announcements
  • Short-form video scripting and production guidance (Reels/TikTok format)
  • Thought leadership articles and long-form LinkedIn content
  • Bilingual content considerations (English and Arabic) as required
3.3   LinkedIn Business Development Support
  • LinkedIn outreach strategy and messaging frameworks targeting orthopedic surgeons, spine specialists, gastroenterologists, hospital procurement managers, and clinical administrators across Saudi Arabia
  • Connection campaign management targeting qualified healthcare professionals and institutional decision-makers
  • Conversation initiating scripts and follow-up messaging templates
  • Lead tracking and pipeline visibility reporting
  • Code-OX shall be considered solely responsible for the full operational management for Mr. Chukri Fadoul's LinkedIn account throughout the Contract Term, conducting all outreach, connection campaigns, and prospect communications on behalf of RIC Medical directly from Mr. Fadoul's account; RIC Medical confirms it has obtained all necessary authorisations from Mr. Fadoul for Code-OX to manage and operate said account
3.4   Brand Identity & Visual Standards
  • Establishment and enforcement of a consistent visual identity framework across all digital touchpoints
  • Colour palette, typography, and graphic standards documentation
  • Photography and content quality guidelines
3.5   SEO & Digital Discoverability
  • LinkedIn Company Page SEO optimisation including keyword strategy, About section, and post tagging
  • Website SEO advisory covering meta descriptions, page titles, and content keyword alignment
  • Google My Business profile setup and optimisation guidance
Note: Services described in this Schedule reflect the strategic scope outlined in the RIC Medical Digital Marketing Strategy Proposal (Ref: COX-RIC-2026-001-P, dated May 2026). 05  ·  Term & Termination
Duration & Termination Rights
5.1   Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date of 5 May 2026 and shall continue for an initial period of twelve (12) months, expiring on 4 May 2027 (the "Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Agreement.

5.2   Renewal

Following the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive periods of twelve (12) months unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal to the other Party at least sixty (60) days prior to the expiry of the then-current term. Renewal terms shall be subject to renegotiated fee structures agreed in writing by both Parties.

5.3   Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon sixty (60) days' written notice to the other Party. In the event RIC Medical terminates for convenience, any amounts owing for Services rendered up to and including the notice period shall remain due and payable. No refunds of prepaid fees shall be made unless agreed in writing.

5.4   Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party: (i) commits a material breach of this Agreement and fails to remedy such breach within fourteen (14) days of receiving written notice of the breach; (ii) becomes insolvent, enters into liquidation, or ceases to carry on business; or (iii) engages in fraudulent, illegal, or grossly negligent conduct.

5.5   Effect of Termination

Upon termination: (i) RIC Medical shall pay all outstanding amounts due to Code-OX within fifteen (15) days; (ii) Code-OX shall deliver all Deliverables produced and paid for up to the termination date; (iii) each Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information of the other Party; and (iv) Sections 7, 8, 9, and 10 of this Agreement shall survive termination.

Mutual Responsibilities
6.1   Code-OX Obligations
  • Perform the Services with reasonable professional skill, care, and diligence
  • Assign suitably qualified personnel to the account
  • Maintain clear communication and provide monthly performance reports
  • Treat all Client information as strictly confidential
  • Comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and platform policies in delivering the Services
  • Notify RIC Medical promptly of any circumstances that may adversely affect delivery of the Services
6.2   RIC Medical Obligations
  • Provide timely access to brand assets, product information, approvals, and feedback required for the performance of the Services
  • Designate an authorised contact person to communicate with Code-OX on all contractual matters
  • Review and approve content within three (3) business days of submission; delayed approvals may affect delivery timelines
  • Pay all fees and the Advertising Budget in accordance with this Agreement
  • Ensure all information provided to Code-OX is accurate, complete, and does not infringe the rights of any third party
  • Comply with applicable laws, including those governing advertising, medical device marketing, and data protection in Saudi Arabia

08  ·  Confidentiality
Non-Disclosure Obligations
8.1   Mutual Confidentiality

Each Party agrees to: (i) keep all Confidential Information of the other Party strictly confidential; (ii) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent; and (iii) use Confidential Information solely for the purposes of this Agreement. These obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

8.2   Exceptions

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (i) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving Party; (ii) was already in the receiving Party's possession prior to disclosure; (iii) is independently developed by the receiving Party; or (iv) must be disclosed by applicable law or court order.

09  ·  Limitation of Liability
Liability Cap & Exclusions
9.1   Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Code-OX's total aggregate liability to RIC Medical under or in connection with this Agreement — whether arising in contract, tort, or otherwise — shall not exceed the total Monthly Retainer fees paid by RIC Medical to Code-OX in the three (3) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

9.2   Exclusions

Neither Party shall be liable to the other for any indirect, consequential, special, incidental, or punitive damages, including but not limited to loss of profits, loss of revenue, loss of business opportunity, or loss of data, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.

9.3   Digital Platform Dependency

Code-OX makes no guarantee of specific commercial outcomes (e.g., sales closed, number of leads generated) as these are dependent on market factors, platform algorithms, and RIC Medical's own sales processes beyond Code-OX's control. Code-OX commits to best-practice execution and the KPI targets outlined in the proposal document.

10  ·  General Provisions
Governing Law & Miscellaneous
10.1   Independent Contractor

Code-OX is and shall remain an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to create an employment relationship, agency, joint venture, or partnership between the Parties. Code-OX's personnel are not employees of RIC Medical and shall not be entitled to any employee benefits from RIC Medical.

10.2   Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall first be subject to good-faith negotiation between senior representatives of the Parties for a period of thirty (30) days. If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to binding arbitration in accordance with the Saudi Arbitration Law (Royal Decree No. M/34, 2012). The seat of arbitration shall be Riyadh, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The language of arbitration shall be English.

10.3   Non-Solicitation

During the Term and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination, each Party agrees not to directly solicit or hire any employee or contractor of the other Party who was materially involved in the performance of this Agreement, without prior written consent.

10.4   Force Majeure

Neither Party shall be in breach of this Agreement or liable for any delay in performing, or failure to perform, any of its obligations if such delay or failure results from circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including but not limited to acts of God, pandemic, war, government restrictions, or failure of third-party platforms. The affected Party shall notify the other Party promptly and both Parties shall cooperate in good faith to mitigate the impact.

10.5   Entire Agreement & Amendments

This Agreement, together with its Schedules and the RIC Medical Digital Marketing Strategy Proposal (Ref: COX-RIC-2026-001-P), constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions, representations, and understandings. Any amendment must be made in writing and signed by authorised representatives of both Parties.

10.6   Notices

All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by email (with read-receipt or acknowledgement) or registered post to the contact details specified in this Agreement. Notices shall be deemed received: (i) upon delivery if sent by registered post; or (ii) upon receipt of acknowledgement if sent by email.

10.7   Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, such provision shall be modified to the minimum extent necessary to make it valid, and the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

10.8   Waiver

No failure or delay by either Party to exercise any right or remedy under this Agreement shall constitute a waiver of that or any other right or remedy. A waiver of any breach shall not be construed as a waiver of any subsequent breach.

10.9   Counterparts

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument. Electronic signatures shall be deemed valid and binding.

01 · Executive Summary

Setting the Strategic Direction

RIC Medical operates at the intersection of global medical innovation and Saudi Arabia's rapidly expanding healthcare sector. This document presents a structured digital marketing strategy designed to elevate RIC's market visibility, engage clinical decision-makers, and generate qualified leads across RIC Medical's full portfolio of advanced medical solutions.

The strategy covers RIC Medical's complete range of medical technologies and equipment categories, positioning the company as a trusted, broad-portfolio technology partner serving hospitals, clinics, and healthcare institutions across the Kingdom — rather than a single-product supplier.

Rather than a fragmented series of campaigns, this blueprint delivers a unified, multi-platform digital presence that builds trust at every touchpoint in the B2B healthcare buyer journey — from initial discovery through to a sales conversation.

🎯

Primary Objective

Position RIC Medical as a trusted healthcare technology partner and generate qualified leads across key clinical segments.

📡

Channel Approach

LinkedIn for B2B lead generation, Instagram for brand credibility, TikTok for educational discovery and awareness.

🏥

Market Focus

Orthopedics, spine care, pain management, gastroenterology, hospital procurement, and surgical centers in Saudi Arabia.

02 · Healthcare Market Context

How Hospitals Discover Medical Technology Today

The way hospitals and clinicians evaluate and procure medical technology has fundamentally shifted. Decision-making is no longer confined to trade shows and in-person sales calls — it begins online, often before a supplier is ever contacted.

Orthopedic surgeons research device efficacy on PubMed and LinkedIn. Gastroenterologists watch procedural technique videos on social platforms. Procurement managers cross-reference suppliers on Google before initiating contact. In Saudi Arabia, Vision 2030's healthcare transformation agenda is accelerating the adoption of advanced medical technologies.

Key Market Insight

A medical technology supplier without a coherent digital presence is effectively invisible to a growing segment of clinical decision-makers who begin their vendor evaluation online. Digital presence is no longer optional — it is a prerequisite for commercial credibility.

14 · Brand Identity & Visual Consistency

The Visual Language of Credibility

In B2B healthcare marketing, visual consistency is not a cosmetic concern — it is a trust signal. Inconsistent branding communicates disorganisation. A coherent, professional visual identity across every platform communicates reliability, quality, and institutional confidence.

🎨

Professional Colour Palette

A defined primary and secondary colour scheme applied consistently to all graphics, social posts, presentations, and documents.

🖼️

Consistent Graphic Templates

Pre-designed templates for LinkedIn posts, Instagram carousels, product highlight graphics, and event announcements.

✍️

Typography Standards

A defined set of fonts applied consistently across digital and print materials. Typography is one of the most overlooked signals of brand professionalism.

📐

RIC Branding Alignment

All content must reflect RIC Medical's existing brand identity — logo usage, brand colours, and brand voice must be consistent across every digital touchpoint.

📷

Photography Standards

Use of high-quality, professional product and clinical photography. Avoid low-resolution or stock images that diminish brand perception.

🗣️

Brand Voice Guidelines

A defined tone of voice — professional, clinically informed, and approachable — applied consistently to all captions, posts, messages, and communications.

15 · 90-Day Marketing Roadmap

A Phased Approach to Launch and Growth

The 90-day roadmap is structured to build the marketing foundation correctly before accelerating into lead generation. Skipping the setup phase in favour of immediate outreach is a common error — a weak digital presence undermines the credibility of every sales conversation that follows.

1
Month 1 — Foundation & Setup
Platform Optimisation and Content Infrastructure
  • Optimise RIC Medical LinkedIn company page — branding, keywords, description, contact information
  • Optimise BDM LinkedIn personal profile — headline, about section, experience, featured content
  • Establish Instagram account with brand-consistent profile, bio, and initial content grid
  • Set up TikTok account and create initial 3–5 educational videos for publishing
  • Define brand colour palette, typography, and graphic template suite
  • Create content calendar for Months 2–3
  • Audit and update website SEO basics (meta tags, page titles, keyword integration)
  • Identify and build initial LinkedIn outreach target list of 100+ decision-makers
2
Month 2 — Content Growth & Audience Building
Consistent Publishing and Community Development
  • Publish LinkedIn posts at minimum 4x per week — technology insights, clinical education, company updates
  • Launch Instagram content schedule — 3–4 posts per week including Reels and carousels
  • Begin TikTok publishing — 3 educational videos per week targeting clinical topics
  • BDM begins LinkedIn outreach — 10–15 personalised connection requests per day to target audience
  • Begin engagement strategy — commenting on relevant industry posts to increase profile visibility
  • Monitor initial content performance and identify top-performing post formats
  • Develop first case study or technology spotlight for LinkedIn
3
Month 3 — Outreach Scaling & Lead Generation
Commercial Conversations and Pipeline Development
  • Scale LinkedIn outreach — BDM initiating 20+ conversations per week with qualified prospects
  • Convert LinkedIn conversations into product demonstration requests and introduction calls
  • Introduce LinkedIn paid promotion for selected high-performing content to expand reach
  • Begin tracking leads: source of discovery, conversion stage, and follow-up timeline
  • Review KPIs against targets and refine content strategy based on data
  • Prepare Month 4–6 roadmap based on learnings from first 90 days
  • Present performance report to RIC Medical leadership with insights and recommendations

The Saudi Arabian healthcare market is in a period of extraordinary transformation. Driven by Vision 2030's healthcare expansion agenda, demand for advanced medical technology is accelerating — and the suppliers who establish digital credibility and clinical relationships today will be the partners hospitals turn to tomorrow.
